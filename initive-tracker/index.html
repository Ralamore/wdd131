Initiative Tracker

Welcome to the Initiative Tracker

So, your party has once again fallen into the dangers of battle. Perhaps they ambushed a group of bandits, perhaps they got caught mid-burglary, or perhaps they finally got on the king's bad side. Whatever they've done, welcome. You're in the right place. This tracker will help you track the initiative any group.

First things first, fill out the forms below with as many players as you have and as many monsters are opposing them. The information will be presented for you to track, or is used in calculating initiative order. Click "submit" when you have finished and it will take you to the tracker page. There, fill out the initiative rolls of each party member and monster, then click the button at the bottom to start the battle. The "Next Turn" button will pass the initiative and the "End Battle" button will drop initiative. Clicking the "Dead" button on the block will remove it entirely. An example player block is below.
